Structuring and identifying the environment is a vital ability among mobile animals. Sense cues are used, leading to path-finding. These sense cues are fundamental to the efficiency and even survival of free moving life.*

Clear image of surroundings is a useful basis of individual growth.

A good environmental image gives its possessor an important sense of security.

Complete chaos without hint of connection is never pleasurable.

The observer should play an active role in perceiving the world, and a creative part in developing his image.
